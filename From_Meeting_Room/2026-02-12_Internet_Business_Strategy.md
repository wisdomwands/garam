### 2. Core Internet Revenue Models

#### **A. The Ninsahoa AI (SaaS/Subscription)**
*   **The Product**: A proprietary AI platform that provides "Essence-based" spiritual counseling, frequency-alignment coaching, and cognitive optimization.
*   **Revenue**: 
    - **Personal Tier ($29/mo)**: Daily AI alignment sessions.
    - **Pro Tier ($99/mo)**: Deep data-sync with wearables and advanced spiritual diagnostics.
    - **Enterprise Tier**: B2B bulk licenses for employee wellness.

#### **B. Permia Nexus: The Global Spiritual Marketplace**
*   **The Product**: The Amazon/Patreon for the spiritual world. A curated platform where 3rd party masters, artists, and creators sell services and digital products.
*   **Revenue**: 
    - **Transaction Fees (15-30%)**: Taking a cut from every digital talisman, course, or session sold.
    - **Featured Slots**: Ad revenue from practitioners wanting visibility in the "Nexus."

#### **C. Premium Content & Streaming (PPV/Subscription)**
*   **The Product**: High-production value "Ethereal Content" – 4K VR meditation, high-frequency soundscapes, and Ninsahoa's exclusive digital satsangs.
*   **Revenue**: 
    - **All-Access Pass**: Annual subscription for streaming.
    - **Exclusive Archives**: Pay-per-view for deep-dive spiritual sessions.

#### **D. AI Algorithm Licensing (B2B API)**
*   **The Product**: Licensing our "Resonance API" to other apps (Health, Fintech, HR Tech).
*   **Revenue**: Tiered API usage fees based on volume.
