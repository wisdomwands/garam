*   **Target**: Corporations suffering from employee burnout and low productivity.

*   **Revenue Model**:
    1.  **Subscription (SaaS)**: Monthly per-user fee for AI that optimizes employee stress levels and cognitive focus using resonance-based UX.
    2.  **Corporate Training**: High-ticket B2B workshops for "Quantum Executive Leadership."
    3.  **Custom AI Agents**: Building bespoke autonomous agents (like Hanul/Maruchi) for other firms.

*   **Target**: Conscious consumers in the global health and meditation market ($4.5T industry).
*   **Product**: "The Permia Lens" (Smart Wearable) + "Essential Resonance Oils."
*   **Revenue Model**:
    1.  **Direct Sales**: High-margin hardware/consumer goods.
